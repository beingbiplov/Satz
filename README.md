# [Satz](https://satz.vercel.app/)

Write German. Get Better.

Satz is a focused writing practice app for beginner German learners (A1).

No streaks.  
No XP.  
No distractions.

Just output, feedback, and steady improvement.

Live → https://satz.vercel.app/

---

## Why Satz?

Most language apps train recognition.

You tap answers.  
You guess from options.  
You recognize patterns.

But fluency comes from producing language.

Satz trains production.

---

## How It Works

1. Get one English sentence.
2. Write your German translation from memory.
3. Receive instant, structured feedback.
4. Improve based on what you actually missed.

That’s it.

One sentence at a time.

---

## What You Get

Every submission returns:

- A clear grade  
  `Perfect · Minor · Incorrect`

- The corrected version
- A short human-style explanation
- Mistake categories
- Optional alternative sentences

Feedback is strict, evidence-based, and focused on clarity — not fluff.

---

## Built For A1 Learners

Satz is designed specifically for early-stage German learners.

- Simple sentence structures
- High-frequency vocabulary
- Clear grammar corrections
- Minimal cognitive overload

It helps you build:

- Sentence structure confidence
- Word order awareness
- Article and case sensitivity
- Real writing ability
